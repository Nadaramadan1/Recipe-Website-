French Vanilla Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1 cup espresso (or strong coffee)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp French vanilla syrup
  • Milk foam
  • Sugar (optional)

Preparation Steps:

  • Brew ☕: Prepare espresso or strong coffee.
  • Heat 🥛: Warm the milk without boiling.
  • Mix 🍯: Add vanilla syrup to the coffee.
  • Pour ✨: Add milk and top with foam.
  • Serve 🎉: Add sugar if desired and enjoy.

Caramel Hazelnut Iced Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold coffee
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 tbsp caramel syrup
  • 1 tbsp hazelnut syrup
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Preparation Steps:

  • Ice ❄️: Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour ☕: Add cold coffee.
  • Flavor 🍯: Add caramel & hazelnut syrup.
  • Mix 🥄: Add milk and stir well.
  • Top 🎂: Add whipped cream if desired.

Arab Coffee

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp Arabic coffee
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Dates (for serving)

Preparation Steps:

  • Boil 🔥: Bring water to boil.
  • Add ☕: Add coffee and simmer.
  • Flavor 🌿: Add cardamom & saffron.
  • Settle ⏳: Let it rest before pouring.
  • Serve 🫖: Pour gently and serve with dates.
