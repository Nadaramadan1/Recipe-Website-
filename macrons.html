Vanilla Bean Macarons

Ingredients

  • Shells: 100g Almond flour, 100g Powdered sugar, 70g Egg whites, 50g Granulated sugar.
  • Filling: 100g White chocolate, 50ml Heavy cream, 1 tsp Vanilla bean paste.

Preparation Steps:

  • Macaronage🥣: Fold dry ingredients into stiff meringue until it flows like lava.
  • Rest⌛: Pipe circles and let them sit for 30-45 mins until a skin forms.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 150°C for 14-16 mins. Let them cool completely before removing.
  • Fill🍦: Sandwich two shells with the creamy vanilla ganache.
♥

Pistachio Macarons

Ingredients

  • Shells: Replace 20g of almond flour with finely ground pistachios.
  • Filling: 80g Pistachio paste, 100g White chocolate ganache.
  • Color: A tiny drop of green food coloring.

Preparation Steps:

  • Combine🥣: Mix the ground pistachios with the almond flour and sugar.
  • Pipe✨: Pipe onto parchment paper and tap the tray to release air bubbles.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 150°C. The "feet" should be stable and not sticky.
  • Nutty Fill🟢: Use the pistachio-infused ganache for a rich, nutty center.
♥

Raspberry Macarons

Ingredients

  • Shells: Classic almond shells with pink food coloring.
  • Filling: Raspberry jam or fresh raspberry reduction mixed with buttercream.

Preparation Steps:

  • Meringue🥣: Whisk egg whites until stiff peaks form, adding pink color at the end.
  • Bake🔥: Same classic method (150°C). Don't let them brown!
  • Tangy Center🍓: Fill with a ring of buttercream and a drop of raspberry jam in the middle.
  • Mature⏳: Store in the fridge for 24 hours for the best chewy texture.
♥
