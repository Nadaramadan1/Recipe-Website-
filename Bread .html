Baguette

Ingredients

  • Flour: 500g All-purpose or Bread flour.
  • Water: 350ml Warm water.
  • Yeast: 10g Active dry yeast.
  • Salt: 10g Fine sea salt

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix & Knead🥣: Combine flour, water, yeast, and salt. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic
  • First Rise⏳: Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1.5 hours until doubled.
  • Shape & Score🥖: Divide dough into long loaves. Let them rise for 45 mins, then make 3 diagonal slashes on top
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 220°C for 25 minutes. Use a tray of water in the oven for a professional crunchy crust.

Natural Sourdough

Ingredients

  • Starter: 100g Active sourdough starter.
  • Flour: 500g High-protein bread flour.
  • Water: 350ml Lukewarm filtered water.
  • Salt: 12g Fine sea salt.

Preparation Steps:

  • Autolyse🥣: Mix starter, water, and flour. Let it rest for 30 mins to develop gluten.
  • Stretch & Fold⏳: Add salt, then perform 4 rounds of folds every 30 mins for a strong structure.
  • Bulk Ferment⌛: Let the dough rise at room temperature for 6-8 hours until bubbly and airy.
  • Bake🔥: Bake in a preheated Dutch oven at 230°C for 20 mins (lid on) and 20 mins (lid off).

Herbed Focaccia

Ingredients

  • Flour: 500g All-purpose flour.
  • Water: 400ml Warm water (High hydration).
  • Yeast & Honey: 7g Instant yeast and 1 tsp honey.
  • Toppings: Extra virgin olive oil, fresh rosemary, and sea salt.

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix & Chill🥣: Mix ingredients into a sticky dough. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  • Dimple & Shape✨: Place in an oiled tray. Use your fingers to create deep dimples in the dough.
  • Garnish🌿: Drizzle generously with olive oil and sprinkle rosemary and flaky salt.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 220°C for 20-25 minutes until the bottom is golden and crispy.
