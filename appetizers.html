Deviled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 6-12 large eggs
  • Mayonnaise : 1/4 to 1/2 cup
  • Mustard : yellow or Dijon, 1-2 tsp
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Paprika for garnish

Preparation Steps:

  • Boil eggs for 10-20 min,then cool in an ice bath before peeling and halving lengthwise.
  • Scoop out the yolks and mash them with wuth mayonnaise, mustared ,salt,pepper until smooth.
  • Spoon or pipe this creamy mixture back into the egg white hollows and finish with a light sprinkle of paprika.

Spinach Artichoke Dip

Ingredients

  • 10 oz frozen chopped spinach
  • 14 oz can artichoke hearts
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Garlic : 1 tsp minced or powder

Preparation Steps:

  • Combine softened cream cheese,sour cream and mayonnaise with chooped spinach and drained artichoke hearts.
  • Stir in shredded mozzarella and Parmesan, then season with garlic powder and red pepper flakes.
  • Bake at 350°F(175°C) for about 25 min until the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned on top.

Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Ibs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo hot sauce
  • unsalted butter : 1/4 to 1/2 cup
  • Garlic powder : 1/2 to 1 tsp
  • Salt & Pepper

Preparation Steps:

  • Pat chicken wings dry and bake them on a wise rack at 425°F(220°C) for about 45-60 min , turning halfway through for even crisping.
  • While they bake, whisk together melted butter and hot sauce in a large bowl .
  • Once the wings are cooked and crispy , toss them in the sauce until fully coated.

Jalapeno Poppers

Ingredients

  • 10-12 fresh jalapenos (halved and seeded).
  • 8 oz cream cheese.
  • 1 cup shredded cheese.
  • Optional: 10-12 slices of bacon (to wrap) or Panko breadcrumbs.

Preparation Steps:

  • Slice Fresh Jalapenos in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes to control the heat.
  • Fill each half with a mixture of cream cheese and shredded cheddar, then top with breadcrumbsor wrap in a half-slice of bacon.
  • Bake at 400°F(200°C) for 15-20 min until the peppers are tender and the topping is golden.
