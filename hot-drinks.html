Hot Chocolate🍫

Ingredients

  • 🥛 2 cups milk
  • 🍫 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 🍯 2 tbsp sugar (or honey)
  • 🍦 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 🍫 Chocolate chips (optional)
  • ☁️ Whipped cream (for topping)

Preparation Steps:

  • Heat 🔥: Warm the milk in a saucepan (do not boil).
  • Mix 🍫: Add cocoa powder and sugar, stir well.
  • Flavor 🍦: Add vanilla extract and mix.
  • Melt ✨: Add chocolate chips and stir until melted.
  • Serve ☕: Pour into a cup and top with whipped cream.

Turkish Tea🍃

Ingredients

  • 💧 ¼ cup water
  • 🍃 5 tbsp tea leaves (for 6 people)
  • 💧 Water for large teapot

Preparation Steps:

  • Prepare 🫖: Pour ¼ cup water in the small teapot and add tea leaves, then cover.
  • Boil 🔥: Fill the large teapot with water and place both pots on the stove. Bring to boil.
  • Simmer ⏳: Reduce heat and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Mix 💧: Transfer more than half of the hot water to the small teapot.
  • Refill 🔄: Fill the large teapot again with water and place them back on the stove.
  • Brew 🍃: Bring to boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  • Serve ☕: Keep warm or serve and reheat when needed.

Herbal Hibiscus Tea🌺🌹

Ingredients

  • 🌺 2 tablespoons dried hibiscus petals
  • 💧 4 cups boiling water
  • 🍯 1 tablespoon honey

Preparation Steps:

  • Boil 🔥: Bring water to a boil.
  • Steep 🌺: Add hibiscus petals and let it steep for 10 minutes.
  • Strain 🍵: Strain the tea to remove petals.
  • Sweeten 🍯: Add honey and stir well.
  • Serve ❄️/☕: Chill for iced tea or serve warm.
