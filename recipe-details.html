Cake 🍰

Course: Dessert

Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Cocoa powder - ½ cup
  • Eggs - 2
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Butter - 100 g
  • Baking powder - 1 teaspoon

Description:

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Pour the mixture into a baking pan and bake in the oven for 30 minutes.

Mahshi (Stuffed Vegetables) 🍲

Course: Main-course

Ingredients

  • Vegetables (Zucchini, Eggplant, or Vine leaves)
  • Rice - 2 cups
  • Tomato sauce - 1 cup
  • Fresh herbs (Parsley, Dill, Coriander)
  • Onion - 2 large
  • Spices (Salt, Pepper, Cumin)

Description:

Mix the rice with herbs, tomato sauce, and spices. Stuff the vegetables with the mixture. Place them in a pot, add broth, and cook over medium heat until tender.
