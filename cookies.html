Ultimate Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • Butter: 200g Browned butter (for extra flavor).
  • Sugar: 1 cup Brown sugar + 1/2 cup White sugar.
  • Flour: 2 cups All-purpose flour + 1 tsp Baking soda.
  • Chocolate: 200g Dark chocolate chunks or pools.

Preparation Steps:

  • Cream🥣: Whisk butter and sugars until fluffy. Add 1 egg and vanilla.
  • Fold Chunks🍫: Gently fold in flour and chocolate chunks. Do not overmix!
  • Chill⏳: Refrigerate the dough for at least 30 mins (This is the secret!).
  • Bake🔥: Scoop onto tray and bake at 175°C for 10-12 mins until edges are golden.
♥

Butter Shortbread Biscuits

Ingredients

  • Butter: 250g High-quality salted butter (softened).
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup Powdered sugar.
  • Flour: 2.5 cups All-purpose flour.
  • Vanilla: 1 tsp Pure vanilla extract.

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix🥣: Beat butter and sugar until creamy. Gradually add flour.
  • Shape✨: Roll out dough and cut into rounds or rectangles. Prick with a fork.
  • Chill⏳: Let them rest in the fridge for 15 mins to keep their shape.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 160°C for 15-20 mins until pale golden. They should melt in your mouth!
♥

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients

  • Oats: 2 cups Old-fashioned rolled oats.
  • Raisins: 1 cup Sweet raisins (soak in warm water for 5 mins first).
  • Spices: 1 tsp Cinnamon + 1/2 tsp Nutmeg.
  • Base: 1 cup Flour, 1/2 cup Butter, 1/2 cup Brown sugar.

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix Dry🥣: Combine oats, flour, and spices in a bowl.
  • Combine✨: Mix with creamed butter and sugar. Fold in the juicy raisins.
  • Scoop🍪: Place large scoops on the baking sheet.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 180°C for 12-14 mins until the centers are still slightly soft.
