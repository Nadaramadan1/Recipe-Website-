Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers(savoiardi):200-300g
  • Mascarpone Cheese:500g
  • Heavy Cream:250ml
  • Espresso Coffee:1cup

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix:Fold the whipped cream into the Mascarpone
  • Dip:Briefly soak ladyfingers in the cold coffe
  • Layer:Alternate soaked biscuits and cream in a dish
  • Chill:Refigerate for 4-6 hours before serving
♥

Black Forest Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Biscuits:250g mixed with 50g melted butter
  • Cream Cheese:500g
  • Heavy Cream:250ml
  • Cherry Pie Filling:1can

Preparation Steps:

  • Crust:Press the crushed biscuis into the bottom of your tin
  • Fill:Mix cream with cheese and spread it
  • Top:Add the cherry filling on the surface
  • Chill:Refigerate for 6 hours before serving
♥

Brownies

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Spread:1 large jar(400g)
  • Eggs:2 large
  • Flour:1/2cup
  • Chocolate Chips:1/2cup

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix:Blend chocolate spread with eggs
  • Fold:Stir in the flour gently with chocolate chips
  • Fill: Pour batter into baking tray.
  • Bake: Cook at 180°C for 25 mins.
♥

Strawberry Cake

Ingredients

  • for cake:self-rising flour:1.5 cups,suger:1cup,eggs:3 large,milk:1/2cup
  • Strawberry Jelly:1 small pack
  • strawberries:1 cup(pieces)

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix:combine flour,suger,eggs,milk,Strawberry Jelly
  • Fold:add the strawberry pieces gently
  • Fill:pour the batter into a greased pan
  • Bake:cook at 180°C for 30-35 minutes
  • Cool:let it rest for 15 minutes before serving
♥

Crème Caramel

Ingredients

  • Milk:2cups
  • Eggs:3large
  • Suger:1/2cup(for cream)+ 1/2cup(for the caramel)
  • Vanila:1tsp

Preparation Steps:

  • Melt:Heat 1/2cup sugar until it turns into Caramel
  • Mix:Whisk milk,eggs,sugar,and vanilla.
  • Fill:Pour caramel in the mold,then add the mixture
  • Bake:Cook in a Water Bath at 160°C for 45 minutes
  • Chill:Refrigerate for 4 hours
♥

Cup Cake

Ingredients

  • Self-Rising Flour:1.5 cups
  • Sugar:3/4cup
  • Soft Butter: 125g
  • Eggs:2large(mixed with 1tsp vanilla)

Preparation Steps:

  • Mix:Whisk butter and sugar until creamy
  • Add:Stir in eggs, then fold in flour
  • Fill:Scoop the batter into cupcake liners(fill to 2/3)
  • Bake:Cook at 180°C for 15-20 minutes
  • Top: Pipe whipped cream on top and Add sprinkles or chocolate chips or black cheery
♥
