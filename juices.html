Pineapple Mango Smoothie 🥭🍍

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 3/4 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/4 cup light coconut milk (plus more if needed)

Steps:

  • Gather all your ingredients.
  • Add mango, pineapple, and coconut milk into a high-powered blender.
  • Blend on high for 1–2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  • If the smoothie is too thick, add a little more coconut milk.
  • Pour into a glass.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your tropical smoothie!

Strawberry Lemonade🍓🍋

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2–3 tablespoons honey or sugar
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  • Gather all your ingredients.
  • Wash and slice the strawberries.
  • Blend the strawberries until smooth.
  • Add lemon juice, water, and honey/sugar.
  • Mix or blend again until well combined.
  • Pour over ice in a glass.
  • Serve cold and enjoy!
