Classic Butter Croissant

Ingredients

  • Dough: 500g Bread flour, 60g Sugar, 10g Salt, 10g Yeast.
  • Butter Block: 250g Cold high-quality unsalted butter.
  • Liquid: 150ml Cold milk + 150ml Cold water.

Preparation Steps:

  • Lamination🥣: Wrap the cold butter block inside the dough. Perform 3 "letter folds".
  • Chill⏳: Chill the dough for 30 mins between every fold to keep the layers intact.
  • Shape🥐: Cut into long triangles, roll tightly, and let them proof for 2 hours.
  • Bake🔥: Brush with egg wash and bake at 190°C for 20 mins until deep golden and flaky.
♥

Pain au Chocolat

Ingredients

  • Pastry: Same laminated croissant dough (above).
  • Filling: High-quality dark chocolate batons (2 sticks per pastry).
  • Glaze: 1 Egg + 1 tbsp milk (Egg wash).

Preparation Steps:

  • Cut✨: Cut the laminated dough into equal rectangles (about 8x12 cm).
  • Assemble🍫: Place one chocolate stick, fold, place the second, and roll.
  • Proof⌛: Let them rise in a warm, draft-free place until they double in size.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 190°C for 18-22 mins. The layers should be visible and crispy.
♥

Strawberry Custard Danish

Ingredients

  • Base: Laminated pastry dough.
  • Custard: 2 Egg yolks, 1 cup Milk, 2 tbsp Sugar, 1 tbsp Cornstarch.
  • Topping: Fresh strawberries and apricot glaze (for shine).

Preparation Steps:

  • Custard🥣: Cook milk, sugar, and starch until thick. Cool completely.
  • Shape🎨: Cut dough into squares, fold corners to center to create a "well".
  • Fill🍓: Pipe a circle of custard in the center and add fresh strawberry slices.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 200°C for 15-18 mins. Brush edges with honey or jam while hot.
