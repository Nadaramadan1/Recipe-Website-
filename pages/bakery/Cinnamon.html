Classic Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • Dough: 500g Flour, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 pack yeast, 1 cup warm milk.
  • Filling: 1/2 cup softened butter, 1 cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp cinnamon.
  • Frosting: 100g Cream cheese, 1/4 cup butter, 1 cup powdered sugar.

Preparation Steps:

  • Knead🥣: Mix dough ingredients and knead until smooth. Let rise for 1 hour.
  • Roll & Fill🌀: Roll out dough, spread butter, and sprinkle cinnamon sugar.
  • Cut & Rise⏳: Cut into rolls and let them rise for another 30 mins.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 180°C for 20-25 mins, then top with cream cheese frosting.
♥

Pecan Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • Dough: Standard brioche-style dough (same as classic).
  • Topping: 1 cup Toasted pecans, 1/2 cup melted butter, 1/2 cup maple syrup.
  • Caramel: 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup heavy cream.

Preparation Steps:

  • Caramel Base🍯: Pour melted butter, brown sugar, and pecans into the baking pan.
  • Place Rolls🌀: Place your filled cinnamon rolls directly on top of the pecan mixture.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 180°C for 25 mins until the caramel is bubbling.
  • Flip Over🔄: Flip the pan immediately after baking so the pecans stay on top!
♥

Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • Dough: Add 2 tbsp cocoa powder to your flour mix.
  • Filling: 1/2 cup Chocolate spread (Nutella), 1/2 cup chocolate chips.
  • Glaze: Melted dark chocolate and a pinch of sea salt.

Preparation Steps:

  • Chocolate Swirl🍫: Spread Nutella instead of butter, then sprinkle chocolate chips.
  • Roll & Cut🌀: Roll tightly to keep the chocolate layers intact.
  • Bake🔥: Bake at 175°C for 22 mins (don't overbake to keep them soft).
  • Drizzle✨: While warm, drizzle with extra melted chocolate for a rich finish.
