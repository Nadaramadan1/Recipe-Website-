Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables: 3 cups
  • Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic: 1 tsp
  • Olive oil: 1 tbsp

Preparation Steps

  • Heat oil, sauté garlic.
  • Add vegetables, stir-fry 5-7 mins.
  • Add soy sauce, serve hot.
♥

Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables: 3 cups
  • Onion & garlic: 1 cup chopped
  • Coconut milk: 1 cup
  • Curry powder: 2 tsp

Preparation Steps

  • Sauté onion & garlic, add vegetables.
  • Add coconut milk & curry powder, simmer 15 mins.
  • Serve with rice or bread.
♥

Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • Quinoa: 1 cup
  • Cherry tomatoes: 1 cup
  • Cucumber: 1 cup
  • Lemon dressing: 2 tbsp

Preparation Steps

  • Cook quinoa, let cool.
  • Mix with chopped vegetables and dressing.
  • Serve chilled or room temperature.
