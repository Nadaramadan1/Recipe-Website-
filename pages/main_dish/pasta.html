Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 200g
  • Bacon: 100g
  • Eggs: 2
  • Parmesan: 50g

Preparation Steps

  • Cook spaghetti al dente.
  • Cook bacon, mix with eggs & cheese, toss with spaghetti.
  • Serve immediately.

Pasta Pesto

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 200g
  • Basil pesto: 3 tbsp
  • Olive oil: 1 tbsp
  • Parmesan: 30g

Preparation Steps

  • Cook pasta, drain and toss with pesto and olive oil.
  • Sprinkle parmesan and serve.

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets: 6
  • Tomato sauce: 2 cups
  • Cheese: 200g
  • Bechamel sauce: 1 cup

Preparation Steps

  • Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese in baking dish.
  • Bake at 180°C for 25-30 mins until golden.
  • Serve hot.
