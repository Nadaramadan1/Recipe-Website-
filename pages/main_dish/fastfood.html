Burger

Ingredients

  • Burger bun: 2
  • Beef patty: 150g
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese: toppings
  • Condiments: ketchup, mustard

Preparation Steps

  • Cook beef patty to desired doneness.
  • Assemble bun, patty, and toppings.
  • Serve immediately.

French Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: 3 large
  • Vegetable oil: for frying
  • Salt: to taste

Preparation Steps

  • Cut potatoes into sticks.
  • Fry in hot oil until golden.
  • Drain, sprinkle with salt, serve hot.
♥

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough: 1 batch
  • Tomato sauce: 1/2 cup
  • Mozzarella: 150g
  • Toppings: veggies or pepperoni

Preparation Steps

  • Roll dough, spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  • Bake at 200°C for 12-15 mins until golden.
  • Serve hot.
