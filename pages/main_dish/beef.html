Beef Steak

Ingredients

  • Beef steak: 400g
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Salt & pepper: to taste
  • Herbs: rosemary

Preparation Steps

  • Season steak with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  • Grill each side 5-7 mins for medium.
  • Serve hot with vegetables or mashed potatoes.

Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • Beef cubes: 500g
  • Carrots, potatoes, onions: 2 cups mixed
  • Beef stock: 2 cups
  • Thyme & bay leaf

Preparation Steps

  • Braise beef with vegetables and herbs.
  • Add stock, simmer 1-2 hours until tender.
  • Serve hot with bread or rice.
♥

Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: 400g
  • Taco seasoning: 2 tsp
  • Soft tortillas: 6
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese: toppings

Preparation Steps

  • Cook ground beef with taco seasoning.
  • Fill tortillas with beef and toppings.
  • Serve immediately.
