Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets: 500g
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Olive oil: 1 tbsp
  • Herbs: thyme & dill
  • Salt & pepper: to taste

Preparation Steps:

  • Marinate salmon with lemon, oil, herbs, salt & pepper.
  • Grill for 6-8 mins on each side until cooked.
  • Serve hot with vegetables or rice.
♥

Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 200g
  • Shrimp: 250g peeled & deveined
  • Cream: 1/2 cup
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • Parmesan: 2 tbsp

Preparation Steps:

  • Cook pasta al dente.
  • Sauté shrimp with garlic, add cream and parmesan.
  • Mix pasta with sauce and serve hot.
♥

Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • White fish: 500g
  • Onion: 1 chopped
  • Coconut milk: 1 cup
  • Curry powder: 2 tsp
  • Chili: 1 tsp

Preparation Steps:

  • Sauté onions, add curry powder, then fish chunks.
  • Add coconut milk, simmer 20 mins.
  • Serve hot with rice or bread.
