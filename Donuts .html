Classic Glazed Donuts

Ingredients

  • Dough: 500g Flour, 1/4 cup Sugar, 1 cup Warm Milk, 1 Egg, 50g Butter.
  • Glaze: 2 cups Powdered Sugar, 1/4 cup Milk, 1 tsp Vanilla.
  • Yeast: 1 tbsp Instant yeast.

Preparation Steps:

  • Knead🥣: Mix dough ingredients and knead for 10 mins until smooth.
  • Proof⏳: Let it rise for 1.5 hours in a warm place until doubled.
  • Cut & Fry🔥: Cut into rings, let rise 30 mins, then fry in hot oil until golden.
  • Dip✨: Dip the warm donuts into the glaze and let them set on a rack.

Rich Chocolate Donuts

Ingredients

  • Dough: Standard donut dough (same as classic).
  • Frosting: 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate chips, 1/4 cup Heavy Cream, 1 tbsp Butter.
  • Topping: Colorful sprinkles or chocolate shavings.

Preparation Steps:

  • Base🍩: Prepare and fry your donuts as usual. Let them cool completely.
  • Ganache🍫: Melt chocolate, cream, and butter together until silky smooth.
  • Frost✨: Dip the top of each donut into the chocolate ganache.
  • Decorate🎨: Add sprinkles immediately before the chocolate sets.

Creamy Filled Donuts

Ingredients

  • Dough: No-hole donuts (round balls).
  • Filling: Strawberry jam, Vanilla Custard, or Nutella.
  • Coating: 1/2 cup Granulated sugar.

Preparation Steps:

  • Shape🍩: Cut dough into solid circles (no holes). Fry until puffy.
  • Sugar Coat✨: Toss the hot donuts in granulated sugar until fully covered.
  • Fill🍓: Use a piping bag with a small tip to poke a hole and fill with jam or custard.
  • Serve😋: Best enjoyed fresh when the filling is still cool and the donut is warm!
